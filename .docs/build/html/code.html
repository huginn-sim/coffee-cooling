coffee.cool(times, t0, dt, T0, Tf, Tp, Te)[source]¶

Calculates a set of Temperature values given some initial conditions and calculated constants.

Parameters:
  • times – A dictionary of time measurements. (‘black’:interval, ‘cream’:samples of ‘black’)
  • t0 – The inital time of Temperature measurement.
  • dt – The time differential.
  • T0 – The initial temperature of the object.
  • Tf – The temperature equilibrium.
  • Tp – The preferred temperature of the object.
  • Te – The immediate change in temperature upon experiment.
coffee.model(t0=0, tf=30, dt=0.1, T0=90, Tf=70, Tp=75, Te=-5, experiments=8)[source]¶

Models the rate of cooling of coffee over time.

Parameters:
  • t0 – The inital time of Temperature measurement.
  • tf – The maximum amount of time allotted for cooling.
  • dt – The time differential.
  • T0 – The initial temperature of the object.
  • Tf – The temperature equilibrium.
  • Tp – The preferred temperature of the object.
  • Te – The immediate change in temperature upon experiment.
  • experiments – The number of intervals to conduct an experiment; add cream.
coffee.sample_data(data=None)[source]¶

Calculates thermal constants from a dataset.

Parameters:data – The dataset used to derive cooling constants. If ‘None’ use default ‘data’.

plot.configure(ax, title, xlabel, ylabel, xbounds, ybounds)[source]¶

Configures the plot with the specified parameters.

Parameters:
  • ax – The current subplot to manipulate.
  • title – The title of the current subplot.
  • xlabel – The label of the x-axis.
  • ylabel – The label of the y-axis.
  • xbounds – The bounds of the x-axis.
  • ybounds – The bounds of the y-axis.
plot.plot(X, Y, suptitle=None, titles=None, xlabels=None, ylabels=None, xbounds=None, ybounds=None, layout='tight', legends=None, custom=None)[source]¶

Plots the specified X,Y data and applies settings. If no ‘custom’ plotting function is defined, all (x,y) in zip(X,Y) are plotted on seperate subplots.

Parameters:
  • X – A list of discrete experiments representing independent variables.
  • Y – A list of discrete experiments representing dependent variables.
  • suptitle – The main title of the figure.
  • titles – The title(s) of the subplot(s).
  • xlabels – The label(s) of the x-axis.
  • ylabels – The label(s) of the y-axis.
  • xbounds – One or two bounds of the x-axis.
  • ybounds – One or two bounds of the y-axis.
  • layout – An argument specifying figure margin presets.
  • legends – Dictionaries specifying the labels of the plot ‘legend’.
  • custom – A user-defined function for plotting data and applying legends.
